privacy notice.

Short version — we collect only what you send us. This is a static brochure site: no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and the site sets no cookies of its own. The data described below is the minimum the open web touches when a page is served, and you can ask for a copy or a deletion at any time.

Who is responsible

The controller is YDT Holdings Oy (Y-tunnus 3608568-3), Kankaistenkatu 22, 13220 Hämeenlinna, Finland — reachable at hello@atheric.eu.

Hosting & server logs

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. To deliver pages and keep the service secure, our hosting provider processes standard technical data on our behalf — such as your IP address, browser user-agent, and request timestamps. This is processed as a service provider (processor) for us; legal basis: our legitimate interest in operating and securing the site (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

If you email us

If you write to hello@atheric.eu, we receive your message and your sending address, and process them solely to read and reply, and to keep necessary records. That is the only direct channel. Legal basis: legitimate interest in replying to people who write to us (Art. 6(1)(f)); if correspondence becomes part of a commission inquiry, the basis shifts to pre-contractual steps at your request (Art. 6(1)(b)).

What we don't collect

  • No analytics — no Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, Umami, or similar.
  • No advertising or marketing pixels.
  • No cookies — the site sets no cookies of its own.
  • No third-party fonts — Archivo, Instrument Serif, and JetBrains Mono are self-hosted under /fonts/.
  • No third-party iframes or embeds.
  • Nothing stored in your browser — the site keeps no local storage or other client-side state.

How long we keep things

Inquiry emails are retained until the conversation has been resolved and a reasonable correspondence-record period has passed (typically twelve months). If you ask us to delete earlier, we will. Server logs are retained per the host's standard policy.

International transfers

Our host, Cloudflare, is based in the United States. Transfer protections rest on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy decision. Email transit may pass through similar infrastructure; the contents are encrypted in transit (TLS).

Your rights

  • Access — ask for a copy of what we hold.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct it.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete it.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing.
  • Portability — receive what we hold in a machine-readable form.
  • Objection — object to processing on legitimate-interest grounds.

Exercise any of these by writing to hello@atheric.eu. We will respond within thirty days.

Complaints

If you believe your data has been mishandled, you can lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman — tietosuoja.fi.
